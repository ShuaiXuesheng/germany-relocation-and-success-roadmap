# Mastering Your New Life in Germany: The Success Roadmap (2026)

This repository provides a structured, step-by-step roadmap for professionals, families, apprentices and students to successfully settle in Germany. It covers the journey from initial preparation to long-term financial stability.

> **Note:** This guide provides informational resources and market comparisons for educational purposes. It does not provide legal visa services or guarantee the issuance of visas.

## 🛂 Phase 1: Pre-Arrival & Visa Preparation
Choosing the right location and securing mandatory documents is the foundation of a successful relocation.
* **Choosing a Location:** Understanding regional differences in cost of living and job markets.
* **Blocked Accounts (Sperrkonto):** Comparing providers to meet visa financial requirements.
* [**Blocked Account Comparison**](https://prepareforgermany.com/en/finances/blocked-account/blocked-account-comparison) 
* **Health Insurance (Incoming):** Mandatory coverage required for the visa application process.
* [**Insurance Guide**](https://prepareforgermany.com/en/health-insurance)

## 🏠 Phase 2: Housing & Initial Setup
Finding a home and setting up your first apartment is the next major milestone.
* **Apartment Search:** Navigating the rental market, Schufa, and viewing appointments.
* **Utilities:** Choosing providers for **Electricity & Gas** to avoid expensive basic supply.
* **Furnishing:** Practical tips for setting up your new home efficiently.
* [**Compare Energy Rates & Housing Tips**](https://prepareforgermany.com/en/housing)

## 📱 Phase 3: Connectivity & Infrastructure
Digital access is essential from day one.
* **Mobile & SIM Cards:** Fast connectivity via Prepaid with **Postident**.
* **Home Internet:** Finding the most reliable **DSL, Cable or Fiber** providers for your address.
* [**Easy SIM & DSL access for Expats**](https://prepareforgermany.com/en/phone-internet)

## 🏦 Phase 4: Long-term Financial Stability
Mastering German bureaucracy and building a secure future.
* **Banking:** Choosing a suitable **Current Account** and avoiding fees.
* **Wealth Building:** Setting up **saving plans (ETFs)** for long-term stability.
* [**Financial Roadmap: From Arrival to Investing**](https://prepareforgermany.com/en/finances)
